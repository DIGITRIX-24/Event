ABOUT THE COLLEGE

Achariya Arts and Science College is a proud symbol of Puducherry a first-grade college established in the year 2004, which is a felicitous outcome of the honourable Managing Trustee Dr. J. Arawindhan of Achariya Educational Public Trust. With 19 years of glorious excellence, the institution has been catering to all the sections of this society by imparting the need-based quality higher education through 10 Undergraduate (UG) programs, 2 Postgraduate (PG) programs, in Arts, Science, Commerce, Economics, and Business Administration. The institution aims at enabling the students especially from the rural neighbourhood to accept new challenges and also imparts education by inculcating the moral values in the young minds. The vision of the institution is to provide need-based, skill-integrated, cost effective, quality and holistic education to transform the students into globally competitive, employable and responsible citizens. It is achieved with the relevant curriculam, pedagogic innovations and various collaborations and also with the best  infrastructurs.

ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The Department of Computer Science is one of the Pioneering Department of the college, The Department of Computer Science had its inception from 2004 and Offering UG Programmes with two Courses such as B.sc Computer Science and BCA. In the year of 2005 B.sc Information Technology Courses has also been initiated. Our Department provides High quality of Education in the Field of Computer Science especially Programming Languages, Problem Solving, Artificial Intelligence, Mobile Applications, Machine Learning and Computer Networking and Internet of Things. It has well equipped laboratories with all the required equipment to do the programming and Web Designing works. The Department regularly organizes Seminars with latest topics like Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science - Big Data Uses, Embedded Systems, Android Projects, 2D & 3D Animation and Internet of Things. Our Department also conducts Workshops on Application Development and Cyber Security. The Faculty Members are motivating the Students both in Academic and Non-Academic field of Education. We provide Knowledge, Awareness Programmes, Seminars, Workshops etc., which enable the students to face the Interviews and get better job opportunity in various  fields.
